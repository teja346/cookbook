Classic Chocolate Mug Cake

Time: 5 min | Serves: 1 | Diet: Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp flour
  • 2 tbsp cocoa
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a mug, add wet ingredients, whisk smooth.
  2. Microwave 60–80 seconds until set. Rest 1 minute and enjoy.

Nutrition (per serving)

  • Calories: 360
  • Protein: 5g
  • Carbs: 48g
  • Fat: 16g
