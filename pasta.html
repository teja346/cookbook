One-Pot Garlic Butter- Pasta

Time: 20 min | Serves: 2 | Diet: Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 cups vegetable stock
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan
  • Salt & pepper
  • Chili flakes (optional)

Steps

  1. Melt butter and sauté garlic for 30 seconds.
  2. Add stock and pasta; cook 10–12 minutes.
  3. Toss with parmesan, season, and serve hot.

Nutrition (per serving)

  • Calories: 520
  • Protein: 14g
  • Carbs: 72g
  • Fat: 18g
