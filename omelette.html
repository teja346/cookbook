Masala Omelette Sandwich

Time: 10 min | Serves: 1 | Diet: Non-Veg

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 onion & 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1 green chili
  • Coriander leaves
  • 2 bread slices
  • Butter
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Beat eggs with veggies and seasoning.
  2. Cook omelette 2–3 minutes.
  3. Butter bread, place omelette inside, toast lightly, and serve.

Nutrition (per serving)

  • Calories: 420
  • Protein: 22g
  • Carbs: 32g
  • Fat: 22g
