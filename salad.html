Zesty Quinoa Salad Bowl

Time: 25 min | Serves: 2 | Diet: Vegan

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 tomato
  • 1/2 red onion
  • Olives
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Whisk lemon juice, olive oil, salt & pepper.
  2. Toss with quinoa and chopped veggies.
  3. Chill 10 minutes, top with olives, and serve.

Nutrition (per serving)

  • Calories: 310
  • Protein: 9g
  • Carbs: 42g
  • Fat: 11g
