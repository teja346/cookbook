Creamy Coconut Chickpea Curry

Time: 30 min | Serves: 3 | Diet: Vegan

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas (rinsed)
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • Spinach (handful)
  • Salt & oil

Steps

  1. Sauté onion until soft; add tomatoes and curry powder.
  2. Add chickpeas and coconut milk; simmer 10–12 minutes.
  3. Stir in spinach to wilt. Serve with rice.

Nutrition (per serving)

  • Calories: 420
  • Protein: 14g
  • Carbs: 46g
  • Fat: 18g
